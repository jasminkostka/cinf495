What is KPOP?

KPOP is short for Korean pop or Korean popular music. The genre is original from South Korea. Its a type of music thats becoming more and more famous. Some Kpop artists even made a song with american singers K-pop is basically a mixer of other genres such as electronic, hip-hop, rock and R&B music.

Another thing about kpop is their Style/Fashion In korea, people seem to like the innocent and feminine look and it is not abnormal for guys to wear makeup in Korea. In fact, its quite common. Another reason why kpop is popular is Dancing almost all kpop group dances unless its a solo artist which is rare.
